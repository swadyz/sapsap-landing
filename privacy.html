SapSap ("we", "our", or "us") is a language-learning social platform. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our app and website, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using SapSap, you agree to the practices described here.

1. What information we collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Account details — your name, username, email address, and password when you register.
  • Profile information — profile photo, bio, and the languages you're learning.
  • Content you create — photos you snap, vocabulary cards, comments, and messages you send to other users.
  • Communications — messages you send to our support team.

Information we collect automatically:

  • Usage data — features you use, lessons you complete, words you learn, and how long you spend in the app.
  • Device information — device type, operating system, app version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Log data — IP address, timestamps, and error logs.
  • Camera and photo data — images you choose to snap within the app. We do not store your camera roll or access photos you haven't explicitly shared with SapSap.

We collect and process photos and images that you choose to upload. These images may include objects, environments, or other people. You are responsible for the content you upload and for ensuring that you have the necessary rights to share such content.

Photos may contain personal data, including identifiable individuals. We do not require users to upload photos of themselves, and users control what content they choose to share.

2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the SapSap app and its features.
  • Personalise your learning experience — tracking vocabulary progress, suggesting words, and adapting lessons.
  • Power social features — showing your posts in the community feed and connecting you with other learners.
  • Send you important notifications about your account, new features, or learning reminders (you can opt out of non-essential notifications at any time).
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms of Service.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We use automated systems, including machine learning and AI-based tools, to analyze images you upload in order to generate language-related suggestions, such as relevant vocabulary and phrases. This processing is necessary to provide the core functionality of the Service.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your photos or other user content to train machine learning models without your explicit consent.

We may use third-party service providers to support image processing and application functionality. These providers process data on our behalf and under appropriate safeguards.

3. How we share your information

We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With other users: Content you post publicly (vocabulary cards, profile) is visible to other SapSap users. You can set your account to private in Settings.
  • Service providers: We work with trusted third-party companies to help run the platform (e.g. cloud hosting, analytics, customer support tools). These providers only process your data on our behalf and under strict data protection agreements.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of SapSap and its users.
  • Business transfers: If SapSap is acquired or merges with another company, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before this happens.

If you choose to share content publicly or with other users, your content (including photos and associated information) will be visible to others within the Service.

You should only upload and share content that you have the right to share. We provide mechanisms for users to report content that may violate their rights or applicable laws. We may review and remove content where appropriate.

4. Legal bases for processing (EEA, UK, and Switzerland)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Performance of a contract: to provide and operate the Service, including account creation, content sharing, and core application features.
  • Legitimate interests: to ensure the security of the Service, prevent fraud and abuse, and improve functionality.
  • Consent: where required, for example for optional features or specific types of data processing.
  • Legal obligations: where processing is necessary to comply with applicable laws.

5. Your choices and controls

You have meaningful control over your data:

  • Access and download: Request a copy of all data we hold about you via Settings → Privacy → Download your data.
  • Edit or delete content: You can edit or delete any post, vocabulary card, or comment at any time.
  • Account deletion: You can permanently delete your account from Settings → Account → Delete Account. All personal data is removed within 30 days.
  • Notifications: Manage push notifications and email preferences in Settings → Notifications.
  • Private account: Switch to a private account so only approved followers can see your posts.
  • Block and report: Block other users or report content at any time from any profile or post.

If you are based in the European Union, you also have rights under the GDPR including the right to access, rectify, restrict processing, and object to processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@sapsap.me.

Depending on your location, you may also have the right to request correction of inaccurate data, restriction of processing, or to object to certain types of processing.

You may exercise your rights by using the features available in the application or by contacting us at the email address provided below. We will respond to such requests in accordance with applicable law.

6. Data retention

We keep your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. If you delete your account, we remove your personal information within 30 days, except where we are legally required to retain it for longer (for example, financial records).

Anonymised, aggregated data (such as overall usage statistics) may be retained indefinitely as it cannot be linked back to you.

We retain user-generated content, such as photos, posts, and comments, for as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide the Service.

If you delete your account, your content will be deleted or anonymized within a reasonable period, except where retention is required for legal, security, or operational purposes.

Backup copies may be retained for a limited period before being permanently deleted.

7. International data transfers

We may process and store your information in countries outside of your own, including the United States.

Where required by applicable law, we implement appropriate safeguards to protect your personal data, such as standard contractual clauses or other lawful transfer mechanisms.

You may contact us for more information about how we transfer and protect your data.

8. Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We use industry-standard measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, access controls, and regular security reviews. However, no system is completely secure — please use a strong, unique password and enable two-factor authentication in your account settings.

If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at security@sapsap.app.

9. Children's privacy

SapSap is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such data, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us at privacy@sapsap.me.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or by email at least 14 days before the changes take effect. Your continued use of SapSap after that date constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please reach out:

  • Email: privacy@sapsap.me
  • Support: Visit our Help Centre

We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 5 business days.
